# HealthPlan


HealthPlan is an innovative health insurance prediction webpage that employs machine learning to forecast insurance costs for individuals. Through a user-friendly interface, HealthPlan provides personalized and accurate estimates based on factors like Age, Gender, BMI,Number of Children,Somker or not and Region. <br> It also features a comprehensive blog section with informative articles on various health insurance plans and helpful tips. <br> The Contact section allows users to connect with the project owner for inquiries or assistance. , <br> With HealthPlan, users can confidently navigate the complexities of health insurance and make well-informed decisions. Experience the convenience and accuracy of HealthPlan as you plan your insurance coverage.
